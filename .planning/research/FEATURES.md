# Features Research: Apartment Locating Lead Gen Website

## Table Stakes (must have)

### Hero Section
- Strong headline communicating the core value prop ("Find Your Perfect Denver Apartment — Free")
- Sub-headline with social proof ("5 years helping Denver renters")
- Primary CTA button ("Get Started" / "Find My Apartment") scrolling to form
- Hero background: full-width apartment/Denver cityscape image

### Lead Capture Form
- All requested fields: name, email, phone, income range, credit range, bedroom count, lease term, location preference
- Clear submit CTA
- Success confirmation message after submit
- Basic validation with user-friendly error messages

### About / Our Story Section
- Alan's story + credentials (5 years Denver experience)
- How the service works (3-step process is effective: Submit → Alan searches → You move in)
- Why use a locator vs searching alone (free service, saves time, insider knowledge)

### Photo Gallery / Visual Section
- Showcase of Denver apartment imagery
- Demonstrates the quality of properties Alan works with

### Contact Info / Footer
- Email address
- Social links (if applicable)
- Simple footer with business name

## Differentiators (builds trust, increases conversion)

### Social Proof
- Number of clients helped ("100+ Denver renters placed")
- Testimonial quotes (even 2-3 are powerful)
- Denver-specific credibility signals

### How It Works Section
- Simple 3-step explainer: "1. Tell us what you need → 2. Alan searches → 3. You tour and sign"
- Reduces friction; visitors understand the process before committing

### FAQ Section (v2)
- "Is this service free?" (yes — locators are paid by the apartment community)
- "How long does it take?"
- "Do you cover all of Denver?"

### Mobile Experience
- Most apartment hunters search on mobile — must look great on small screens

## Anti-Features (deliberately NOT for v1)

| Feature | Why Not |
|---------|---------|
| Apartment listing/search | Not a listings site — Alan hand-curates options |
| Map integrations | Complexity, not needed for lead capture |
| Live chat | Not needed; form is sufficient |
| Blog/content | Scope creep for v1 |
| User accounts/login | No reason to authenticate visitors |
| Availability calendar | Not applicable to this service model |
| Price range filters | Alan provides this during consultation |
