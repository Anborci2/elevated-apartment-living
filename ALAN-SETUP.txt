-----------------------------------------------------
HOW IT ALL WORKS — THE BIG PICTURE
-----------------------------------------------------
Think of your website as having four jobs running
quietly in the background at all times:

  1. SHOW UP
     Your site is live 24/7 at your domain. Anyone
     who finds you online lands on a professional page
     that represents your business.

  2. CAPTURE LEADS
     When a visitor clicks "Find My Apartment" they
     fill out a short form. The moment they hit submit,
     you get an email with all their details — name,
     budget, neighborhoods, timeline — ready for you
     to follow up.

  3. BUILD TRUST AUTOMATICALLY
     Every night the site checks your Google reviews.
     Any 4 or 5 star review gets sent to your approval
     dashboard. You review it, click publish, and it
     appears on your site. Bad reviews never make it
     through. No manual work needed.

  4. STAY CURRENT
     You have a private dashboard only you can access.
     Log in anytime to add reviews, update content, or
     make changes — no technical knowledge required.

The accounts below are what power each of these jobs.
Most people will never know they exist — they just
keep everything running behind the scenes.
