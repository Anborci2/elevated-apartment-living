HOW TO PUBLISH A CHANGE
-----------------------------------------------------
1. Open the Studio and log in
2. Click the section you want to change
3. Edit the text or upload a photo
4. Click the "Publish" button (bottom of the screen)

IMPORTANT: "Publish" means it goes live. Your change
appears on the real website within about an hour.
If you don't click Publish, nothing changes.

Tip: make a small edit and check the site an hour
later the first time, just so you see how it works.
